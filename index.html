About Temple University MCGSA

The Media and Communication Graduate Student Association (MCGSA) at Temple University represents and advocates for the collective interests of M&C PhD graduate students in all matters of policy and action. Located in Philadelphia at Klein College of Media and Communication, MCGSA seeks to build community among graduate students within the Media and Communication Department and to facilitate connections between M&C graduate students and students and faculty across disciplines. Above all, MCGSA works to optimize the personal and professional development of graduate students through resources, events, and academic support.
