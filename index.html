About Us

The Media and Communication Graduate Student Association (MCGSA) at Temple University represents and advocates for the collective interests of M&C graduate students in all matters of policy and action. The MCGSA seeks to build community among the graduate students within the Media and Communication Department and to facilitate connections between M&C graduate students and students and faculty across disciplines. Above all, the MCGSA works to optimize the personal and professional development of graduate students.
